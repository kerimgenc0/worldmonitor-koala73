Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

World Monitor (“we”, “our”, or “the service”) provides real-time global intelligence: maps, news aggregation, insights, and related data via web and mobile apps. We are committed to transparency about data practices.

We do not store your personal data

We do not maintain user accounts, profiles, or personal databases. We do not collect names, emails, or other identifying information from users of the public service or the iOS app.

What we process

  • API and server requests. When you use the app or website, our servers receive requests (e.g. map data, insights). We may retain minimal technical data such as IP address, timestamp, and endpoint for a limited time for security and operations. This is not linked to any identity.
  • Analytics. We may use privacy-focused analytics (e.g. Vercel Analytics) for aggregate usage and performance. Such tools are configured to avoid collecting personally identifiable information.

iOS app and in-app purchases

Purchases in the World Monitor iOS app are handled by Apple and RevenueCat. Payment and subscription data are processed according to Apple’s Privacy Policy and RevenueCat’s Privacy Policy. We do not store payment details or account credentials.

Data we do not sell

We do not sell, rent, or trade user data to third parties for advertising or marketing.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For privacy-related questions: support@worldmonitor.app.
